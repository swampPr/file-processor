Compress an MP4 video

Here you can upload an MP4 video and have it compressed. You can choose between our default option and our aggressive compression option. Please note that compression may take a while, especially if the file is large, or if the aggressive option is enabled. The default compression will maintain an even balance between file size and quality, while the aggressive compression will reduce the image and audio quality to a noticeable but still usable amount. Only files below 500mb can be processed.
